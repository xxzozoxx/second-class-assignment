Who are we?

is the first Iraqi based company that is specialized in the collection and purification of used cooking oils.Utilizing our purpose built storage and processing facility, and through our highly committed team, we work hard to ensure the proper implementation of used cooking oil collection services and utilization in internationally approved green initiatives.

Our mission

To create a pollution free environment for the coming generations in Iraq. Through the implementation of international standards in recycling waste materials.

Our vision

To collect and recycle as much waste material as possible. And become the pioneering recycling company in Iraq, by providing professional Business to business solutions.

Why Recycle?

Cooking oil that is poured down the sink or drain ends up in our waterways where is becomes an environmental hazard. It can also block drains and choke up sewer systems and water treatment facilities. Instead, used cooking oil can be effectively recycled into a number of secondary products including bio-diesel. Bio-diesel is a clean burning fuel alternative to petroleum diesel and helps reduce reliance on petroleum products and reduce emissions from the transport sector.

The Recycle Procedure:

Supply

Our local drivers supply premium cooking oils right to your door and they can also collect your used cooking oil at the same time. Find out more about our range of quality cooking oils.

Collect

Our drivers are registered to collect both used cooking oil and food waste. We have worked hard to develop a reliable, award winning service that takes the time and hassle out of recovering your organic waste.

Convert

Everything we collect is recycled or converted into renewable energy; nothing goes to landfill. We do this through our network of regional depots and by applying our advanced recycling knowhow.
